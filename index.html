The Technology

Nature creates what technology cannot copy

Every agate, jasper, or quartz slice carries a microstructure formed over millions of years — a pattern unique to that single specimen in the entire universe.

JadeKey reads this pattern, converts it into a cryptographic identifier, and permanently links it to your artwork. The stone becomes the key. The artwork becomes verifiable.
